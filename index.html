Survival Tip!

Farming
Always need wards so you will have a sight while farming you will be alerted when an enemy hero might gank you.
Supporting
When supporting always buy wards for the team and always dewards so the enemy have no sight of your team bur courier and upgrade courier as soon as possible always protect the Carry on the team.

Carry
If your role is a carry you need to build offensive items as soon as possible always be there when clash star's your team weill need you as an offensive killer.

Tank/Initiator
when your role is being a tank you need to always at front or the starter of the clash and the one that would need to take ost of the damage you would need to have defensive items for the team to have a hard tank hero.
